### P4-3: PodDisruptionBudget Auto-Generation

**Status:** 🟡 Partial (Infrastructure concept ready, implementation deferred)

**Concept:** Generate PDB per pool to enforce availability guarantees:
- Example: apps-ha pool → ensure ≥1 of {synapse, nexus, quanta} nodes available
- Example: platform-ha pool → ensure ≥2 of {thalamus, cerebellum, cortex, cerebrum, corpus} available

**Why Deferred:**
- PDBs protect Pods, not Nodes (protect workload availability, not infrastructure)
- More effective at workload level than pool level
- Would require per-namespace PDBs, not per-pool
- Better handled via individual workload SLAs (P5)
